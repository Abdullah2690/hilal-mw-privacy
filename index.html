Hilal MW — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Hilal MW ("the App") is an Islamic calendar and prayer companion app built for Muslims in Malawi. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data: With your permission, the App uses your device's GPS location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. You can decline location access and manually select your district instead.
  • Device notification token: Used to deliver prayer time reminders and announcements from Ruuyatl Hilal, via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Feedback submissions: If you submit a bug report or suggestion through the App, we store the message you provide and, optionally, a contact email if you choose to share one.
  • Donation information: If you choose to donate, payment is processed directly by our payment partner, Paychangu. We do not store your card, mobile money number, or any payment credentials on our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not require you to create an account to use its core features — prayer times, the Hijri calendar, Qibla direction, and announcements are all available without any sign-up. We do not collect your name, phone number, or contacts.

3. How Information Is Used

  • Location is used only to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area.
  • Notification tokens are used only to deliver prayer reminders and official announcements.
  • Feedback is used only to improve the App and respond to reported issues.

4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notification delivery
  • Paychangu — donation payment processing

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing data they process on our behalf.

5. Data Retention

Location data is used in real time to calculate prayer times and is not stored on our servers. Feedback submissions are retained to help us track and resolve issues, and may be deleted periodically.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the feedback option inside the App.
